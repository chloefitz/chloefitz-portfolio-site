Project: Talkmap Graphics

Project Type:

Editorial Design • Infographics


Technologies:

Adobe InDesign • Adobe Illustrator • Adobe Photoshop

01 BKG

Talkmap is a SaaS company working in the conversational intelligence space. They provide conversational analytics for their clients via their AI platform. I produced a range of graphic content for Talkmap, including infographics for reports and research, and editorial content like the sell sheet included here.

* Note - Written content in these graphics has been blurred to protect information pertaining to the client






02 DESIGN

When designing presentation material, especially content meant to communicate research or data, clarity and balance are key. The infographics need to be visually appealing, but also clean enough that the visuals aren't distracting to the viewer. Balancing white space with a clear, high contrast, accent color ensures clarity and readability.




03 RESULT

The content pictured here was used across content for the company. The sell sheet document was not only presented to upper level management, but was also used in discussions with key stakeholders. Similarly, the infographics were included as part of a larger market research presentation which was also presented to company executives.
