Hi, I'm Chloe Fitzgibbons

Nice to meet you! I am currently a Marketing Consultant with MachineBlack. In this role, I've had the chance to work on both strategic and visual projects with a range of clients.

I recently graduated Summa Cum Laude from Boston College with a major in Communication and minors in Marketing and French. Through exploring these areas academically and professionally, I've grown particularly interested in the ways in which visual media can impact viewers. This interest has put me on the exciting path of visual design and communication.

When I create, whether it's generating marketing collateral, developing client branding, or animating videos, my goal is always to connect with whoever views my work. I'm excited to connect with you!
