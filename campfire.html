Completed as a part of the course Intermediate Digital Art: Animation at Boston College, the purpose of this assignment was to experiment with physical media for stop motion animation. In the assignment, we were responsible for creating a small scene using materials of our choice. Then, we also undertook the sound design and editing of the sounds over our captured stop motion footage.



02 DESIGN

I chose to work with cut paper for this project.
