Client: Boston College Center for Corporate Citizenship


Project Type:

Editorial Design • Presentation Design • Social Media


Technologies:

Adobe InDesign • Adobe Illustrator • Adobe Photoshop



01 BKG

The Boston College Center for Corporate Citizenship is an extension of the Boston College Carroll School of Management. The Center is a corporate membership organization focused on advancing corporate social responsibility (CSR) and ESG education, performance, and disclosure. They produce a range of content for the public and for members, in addition to hosting their annual Corporate Citizenship Conference. For the period of 2021-2023, I was the Design and Marketing Assistant for the Center, a role in which I produced a range of editorial (digital and print), presentation, social, and other graphic and written content.




02 DESIGN

The Center had a clear branding guide for their overall content. This position helped me gain the necessary skills to quickly adapt to organizational branding guides, while still knowing how to innovate and keep designs clean, clear, and engaging. When designing for the Center, I worked closely with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment across all projects. Many of the designs displayed here represent the result of detailed discussions with relevant stakeholders to ensure that I was able to visually communicate their intended message.




03 RESULT

While working within established branding guides, I had the chance to innovate significantly and add to the organization's style guides, implementing best practices that are still in use today. Over the course of my involvement with the Center, I worked on the production of 10 magazine issues, 20+ reports, materials for 3 annual conferences, and a range of additional collateral for the Center's website and social channels.
