Project: NBSO Explainer Video Animation
K-12 Music Keyword Equity Database


Project Type:

Animation


Technologies:

Adobe AfterEffects • Adobe Illustrator

01 BKG

The New Bedford Symphony Orchestra (NBSO) is an organization committed to providing exciting, enriching, and transformative classical music experiences to audiences of all ages. Outside of their concert programs, a large focus for NBSO is their educational branch, particularly centering on supporting both K-12 students and educators.

Within their educational arm, the NBSO realized a significant gap in database technologies that were specifically focused on BIPOC composers and repetoire. So, they decided to develop the first-of-its-kind, free to access and use, "K-12 Music Keyword Equity Database" that provides educators, or anyone interested, a continually updating and easily searchable database of BIPOC composers and pieces to broaden the listening examples used in music classes. They developed this great technology, then the question became: how do we show people what this can do?




02 DESIGN

When designing for this project, I worked closely with NBSO representatives to discuss their desired tone and overall aesthetic for the video. We aligned on the fact that it should be something fun, upbeat, and snappy in order to keep a viewer's attention, while also being clear, clean, and simple enough to clearly communicate the unique and powerful capabilities of this database. We settled on a mix of animation of recorded media to use animated elements to highlight the actual look and feel of the database platform itself.





03 RESULT

This project resulted in a short explainer video detailing the functionality and goals of the NBSO's database platform. This video was able to be used on the NBSO YouTube channel in order to promote the database, and was used as an ad hoc reference to which prospective educators and interested parties were directed towards. The NBSO representatives felt that this video really captured the desired tone and accomplished the goal of clearly showcasing the database in an engaging way.
